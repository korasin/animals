The kiry Animal shelter

"The Kiry is an animal welfare charity organization in Kery that deals for the most part with domestic animals.

It is the only animal welfare organization that runs an animal shelter in Kery. "We are a dedicated team of cat lovers in Narabi that rescues, rehabilitates and socializes kittens.
Kittens in our sheller are dewormed, treated for fleas, litter trained and most importantly Vaccinated prior to adoption.

The society has branches in Naraba, Mamba and Nvasaiha.
